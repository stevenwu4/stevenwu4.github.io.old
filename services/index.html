Courses I've TA'd (parantheses is # times):

  • Linear Algebra with Applications to Business

  • Elementary Mathematics for Economics I
  • Linear Algebra I
  • Elementary Calculus I (x2)
  • Business Statistics I
  • Business Statistics II (x4)
  • Introduction to Statistical Modeling I (x2)
